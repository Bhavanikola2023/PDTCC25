Meet Your AI Assistant for Higher Ed Admins

FERPA-Aware. Precision-First. Built for Real Work.

Streamline administrative tasks, ensure compliance, and reclaim valuable time with an AI assistant designed specifically for higher education professionals.

Launch Admin GPT

User:

"I need help drafting an email to faculty about the upcoming accreditation visit."

Admin Assistant:

I'll help you draft that email. Before I begin, could you provide me with:

  • Key details about the accreditation visit (date, time, which accreditor)
  • Specific actions faculty need to take to prepare
  • Your preferred tone (formal, conversational, urgent)

This information will help me create a FERPA-compliant, effective communication.

How It Works

The "Ask Before Act" Principle

This GPT never answers without asking questions first. It clarifies goals, audience, format, and compliance boundaries (FERPA)—then confirms with the user before producing a single output.

Clarify Intent

The GPT asks targeted questions to understand the full context and requirements of your request.

Assess Compliance

Privacy regulations like FERPA are built into its reasoning process, ensuring your work stays compliant.

Confirm Approach

Before delivering the final output, the GPT confirms its understanding and approach with you.

Deliver & Refine

After confirmation, it delivers highly customized results that can be further refined based on your feedback.

Focus: Leveraging AI for daily admin tasks

Suggest ways I can use generative AI to streamline my administrative workload. Include ethical concerns and tools suitable for higher ed compliance (FERPA/ADA).

Focus: Personal/professional growth through proactivity

Prompt:

Coach me on how to show more initiative in my admin role. Give examples relevant to higher ed and include a mini action plan.
3. Prompted to Perform – GenAI for Next‑Level Admin Support

Focus: Writing better prompts for admin use‑cases

Prompt:

Help me craft reusable prompts for tasks like scheduling, email drafts, and data entry—tailored for university administration.
4. Elevate Your Impact – Communication Strategies

Focus: Sharpening communication styles

Prompt:

Evaluate my email draft for tone and clarity, and suggest how to make it more inclusive and impactful for university staff.
5. Time Management

Focus: Planning and productivity

Prompt:

Create a prioritized schedule for my week, considering meetings, project deadlines, and time for email catch‑up.
6. Leveraging AI to Engage and   Empower

Focus: Personalized AI in student/staff engagement

Prompt:

Suggest ways I can use AI tools to personalize student communications while maintaining FERPA compliance.
7. Cultivating a Growth Mindset

Focus: Adopting resilience and learning

Prompt:

Create a reflective journal prompt that encourages a growth mindset for an administrative professional overcoming work challenges.
8. Big Picture Thinking

Focus: Strategic decision‑making

Prompt:

Help me map a current process (e.g., budget approval or faculty onboarding) to a strategic overview with dependencies and long‑term impact.
9. Valuing a Multi‑Generational  College

Focus: Navigating intergenerational dynamics

Prompt:

Generate best practices for cross‑generational communication among faculty, staff, and students at a university.
10. Writing for Success – Effective Email Communications

Focus: Email etiquette and formatting

Prompt:

Revise this email for clarity, tone, and professionalism. Make sure it follows higher‑ed etiquette and is ADA‑friendly.
11. Customer Service Excellence

Focus: Handling difficult people and service quality

Prompt:

List common student or faculty service complaints and offer AI‑based templates or responses to address each scenario.
12. Upgrade Your Organizational  Skills

Focus: File, time, and space management

Prompt:

Design a file‑naming and folder structure system for managing student records, project files, and meeting notes.
13. Maximize the Power of  MS Teams

Focus: Communication through MS Teams

Prompt:

Give me a quick‑start guide for setting up, running, and following up on a virtual meeting in MS Teams, including accessibility reminders.
14. Meeting Lifecycle  Management

Focus: Planning and documenting meetings

Prompt:

Draft a meeting agenda and follow‑up email for a department meeting that includes updates, action items, and compliance reminders.
15. Slide Decks or Visuals for  Session Recaps

Focus: Post‑session content delivery

Prompt:

Summarize this session into a 3‑slide presentation with headings, key points, and follow‑up actions in plain language.
16. Workshop Recap Newsletter

Focus: Wrapping up the event for internal communication

Prompt:

Create a 1‑paragraph recap for each of today’s sessions, formatted for inclusion in a newsletter to university staff.
17. Workshop Feedback &  Improvement

Focus: Collecting feedback & iterating

Prompt:

Write 5 quick questions for a post-event feedback form that evaluates content, delivery, and AI session usefulness.
